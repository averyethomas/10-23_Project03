About the Data

All of the data was obtained from the U.S. Department of Labor's Bureau of Labor Statistics, which is the Federal agency responsible for measuring labor market activity, working conditions, and price changes in the economy. It's goal is to collect, analyze and disseminate essential economic information to support public and private decision-making. As an indpendent statisitcal agency, BLS serves its diverse user communities by providing products and services that are objective, timely, accurate, and relevant.
